REPORT READINESS SCORING RUBRIC
================================
Evidence (25 pts): confirmed request/response, screenshot, reproduction steps
Impact (25 pts): demonstrable business harm, quantified if possible
Reproducibility (20 pts): working PoC script, consistent results
False Positive Risk (20 pts): ruled out scanner artifacts, verified manually
Writeup Quality (10 pts): clear title, summary, CVSS, remediation
--------------------------------
>= 80: Submission-ready
60-79: Needs minor evidence or writeup improvements
40-59: Requires deeper verification
< 40: Not ready for submission
